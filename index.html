ABOUT US

Who We Are & Where We're From

We’re a team of music creators hailing from Texas and Finland, bringing together two distinct cultures and a shared passion for sound. Starting out with FL Studio and whatever gear we could find, we’ve spent years honing our skills in both hemispheres, inspired by the sounds and styles of our unique backgrounds. From humble beginnings to working with artists around the world, we’re here to bring that same creative energy and expertise to every track we touch.

Vocal Mixing & Mastering

At our studio, we specialize in taking your raw vocals to the next level. With a tailored approach to vocal mixing and mastering, we ensure your sound is clean, crisp, and radio-ready. Whether you’re a solo artist or part of a band, we treat every track with precision and care, delivering professional results that make your music shine.

Songwriting & Music Consultation

Looking for help with songwriting or need some feedback on your latest project? We offer personalized songwriting services, collaborating with you to bring your creative ideas to life. Additionally, our one-on-one video consultations are perfect for discussing anything music-related, from vocal techniques to production tips. We’re here to support your musical journey every step of the way.
